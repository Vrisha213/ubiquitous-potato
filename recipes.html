SMOOTHIES



MANGO SMOOTHIE

Ingredients
1 cup non-fat milk 1 cup greek yogurt 1 ½ cups ice cubes 1 banana 1 mango ⅛ cup unsweetened coconut 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 tablespoon honey chia seeds
Method
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth, about 3 minutes. Serve with chia seeds, additional coconut, and chopped mango if desired.

GREEN SMOOTHIE

Ingredients
2 cups spinach 2 cups water 1 cup mango 1 cup pineapple 2 bananas
Method
Tightly pack 2 cups of leafy greens in a measuring cup and then toss into blender. Add water and blend together until all leafy chunks are gone. Add mango, pineapple and bananas and blend again until smooth. Pour into a mason jar (or cute cup of your choice). Gulp or sip like a rawkstar!

COFFEE SMOOTHIE

Ingredients
1 cup strong brewed coffee 1 banana 1/4- cup rolled oats 1 tablespoon cocoa powder 1 tablespoon flaxseeds meal 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1 cup soy milk or almond milk 1 teaspoon honey
Method
Pour coffee into an ice cube tray and freeze overnight. Combine all the ingredients in a blender, including the coffee ice cubes and blend until smooth. Taste for sweetness and adjust accordingly. Serve.


TROPICAL SMOOTHIE

INGREDIENTS
1 orange, peeled and quartered 1 ripe banana, peeled and sliced 1 cup coconut water 1 cup frozen mango chunks 1 cup frozen pineapple chunks 1 teaspoon chia seeds
Method
Add all ingredients to a blender, and blend until smooth. Serve immediately.

CHOCOLATE SMOOTHIE

Ingredients
3 pitted dates 1 cup unsweetened chocolate almond milk (such as Almond Breeze) 1 cup ice 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder 1 container vanilla soy yogurt 1/2 banana, sliced and frozen Dash of instant coffee or espresso granules
Method
Place dates in a small bowl; cover with hot water. Let stand 3 minutes or until softened; drain. Place milk and dates in a blender; blend 30 seconds or until pureed. BERRY SMOOTHIE

Ingredients
1 banana 1 c. frozen strawberries 1 c. frozen blackberries, plus more for garnish (optional) 1 c. frozen raspberries 1 1/4 c. almond milk 1/2 c. Greek yogurt
Method
In a blender, combine all ingredients and blend until smooth. Divide between 2 cups and top with blackberries, if desired.


ORANGE SMOOTHIE

Ingredients
2 ripe bananas, peeled and cut into 1 inch pieces 1 cup orange juice 1 orange, peeled and cut up into chunks ¾ cup almond milk 1 tsp vanilla extract 1 carrot, peeled and cut up into 1 inch pieces Ice
Method
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend to combine. (If your blender has a smoothie/frozen drinks setting use that)

WATERMELON SMOOTHIE

Ingredients
2 cups frozen cubed watermelon 1 cup water 1 tablespoon of honey or other sweetener to taste a few mint and/or basil leaves
Method
Blend the watermelon and the water until smooth. Add the honey and mint and blend another 10-20 seconds until mostly smooth and the consistency of a slushy. Serve immediately!

POMEGRANATE SMOOTHIE

Ingredients
1 cup crushed ice 2 bananas , sliced 1/2 cup nonfat plain yogurt 1/2 cup pomegranate juice 2 tablespoons honey 3 tablespoons pomegranate seeds for garnish
Method
Combine ice, banana slices, yogurt, pomegranate juice and honey in a blender and blend until smooth. Add more yogurt or juice as necessary, depending on your desired consistency, and adjust sweetener to your taste. Pour into two glasses and garnish with pomegranate seeds. Serve.
